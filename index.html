HOW INFORMATION GETS STOLEN

Recognise the attack before it works

Cybercriminals often target human trust before they target technology.

↗

Suspicious websites, apps & links

Fake login pages, cloned shops, malicious downloads and shortened links can steal passwords or install harmful software.

Check domain spelling, permissions and the official source.
⌁

Weak device protection

Outdated systems, reused passwords, disabled security tools and unsafe public Wi-Fi make information easier to access.

Update devices and use unique passwords with 2FA.
◉

Impersonation calls & messages

Scammers pretend to be banks, insurers, police, delivery services, employers, friends or family members.

End the conversation and verify independently.
PREVENTION PLAN

Three checks can stop many scams

01

Stop and inspect

Look for urgency, fear, rewards, unusual payments and unknown links.

02

Verify independently

Use the official app, website or phone number—not details supplied by the sender.

03

Protect and report

Block the sender, preserve evidence, change exposed passwords and contact the bank quickly.
